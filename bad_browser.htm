Why should I upgrade?
Many webpages, including this one, will not work on outdated browsers. Also, modern browsers are faster, display web pages better, have less security problems (that's right, your browser could be insecure and hackable!), and have more features.
What are my options?
If you prefer a browser with lots of add-ons and user generated content, try FireFox

If you want a browser that is blazingly fast, stable, and has built in email, then I suggest Opera

If you prefer a simple browser and don't care about speed and security, get Internet Explorer
